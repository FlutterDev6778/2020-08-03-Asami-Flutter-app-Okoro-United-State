**UserApp Screens**

- Splash Screen- First screen shown when entering the app.
- Home Screen-Webview of your Mobile Website.
- User Account
   - Personal Info 
   - Profile Picture
   - Contact Info
   - Posts
- Members Screen-Access to all the promo codes on your shop
- The member can see exclusive promotions & sales going on.
- News Feed-Customers & Admin can post content
- Post Feed
- Travel Link to Trip Planning Tools
- Interactive Map: Each Country will Have Markers when the markers are pressed a screen pops up displaying the different teas. Then there will be an explore country screen that takes users to a detailed screen. Which display attractions, fun facts,about the country
- Features
   - Subscription under Monthly Tea Packages, when someone becomes a member they get tea shipped automatically to their house. 

**Admin App**

- Login
- Post to Feed
- Add Current Promotions
- Push Notifications page to send notifications to customers.
